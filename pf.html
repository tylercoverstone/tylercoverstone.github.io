PackFinder


In September of 2016, a peer asked me if I could make a prototype of an IOS application for his start-up company as something that he could show potential investors and to build upon as his ideas grow. The resulting PackFinder prototype application that I created was written in Swift, and took loosely less than a month to complete. The idea for the application is a social environment for car enthusiasts to create profiles, reach out and communicate, share locations of car meets, and promote individuality through a shared interest.
